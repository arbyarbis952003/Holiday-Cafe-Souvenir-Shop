About Us

Holiday Cafe and Souvenir Shop is a medium-sized company focusing on serving foods and drinks, as well as souvenirs and decorations depending on the holiday being celebrated.

It is a cafe that follows a theme based on different holidays in the Philippines and around the world. Holiday Cafe and Souvenir Shop is a medium-sized company in the Philippines. Born as a dream of its owners who love enjoying holidays, it is a cafe that adapts its theme and ambiance based on the different holidays in the Philippines and around the world. It has currently 500 baristas, pastry chefs, and cooks, 500 cashiers and other crews, 150 store managers, distributed to the twenty-five (25) branches and an estimate of 300 head office workers including an IT Department. It offers staple and seasonal foods and drinks for its customers whether dine-in, take-out, or delivery and pick-up via third party companies. Additionally, it also sells souvenirs and decorations depending on the holidays being celebrated here and abroad. Holiday Cafe and Souvenir Shop operates from Monday to Sunday from 6:00 AM - 12:00 MN including all holidays in all branches. It was recently awarded to be one of the go-to companies for generous benefits and compliance to the labor law and other related national policies.
